Catering

We provide catering services to all our valuable guests. Our kitchen staff cooks fresh food which is not only delicious but also made in a hygienic environment using high quality ingredients. You can choose any items of your choice from our menu. You can also order dishes that are not in the menu and our staff will freshly cook as per your requirements. From breakfast, lunch to dinner, our farm house staff is ready to prepare meals for your guests. We offer you a complete one-stop solution, providing you with everything you need to make your event complete. You can enjoy deliciously cooked beef and chicken tikka, kababs and biryani in the dining areas of our farm house or even outside the blue shades of night environment. The food can also be pre-heated in our kitchen area to revive the taste.
